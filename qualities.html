Five Qualities

  1. Home
  2. Five Qualities

Collaboration

  • How well you work with others. It can be in group projects or in real life situations.

Communication

  • How well you can express your thoughts and ideas to others.

Creativity

  • How well you can create ideas, rather than how well you create them or how good the idea is.

Inquiry

  • How well you can ask questions, and properly formulate a question to get the answer you need.

Persistence

  • Trying over and over until you succeed. But knowing when to stop is also a good trait.
