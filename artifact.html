Coding Progression

On this page I will inform you of what I learned in Coding. I learned many tags in HTML and CSS. I also learned a lot about webpage aesthetics. This year, I learned a lot about coding and now page looks even better than my page I made in 6th and 7th grade.

Some of the tags that I learned were:

TagWhat It Does
<strong>Best practice for bolded text.
<em>Best practice for italicized text.
<a>Makes an anchor to another website or different part of the webpage.
<p>Creates a paragraph.
<ul>Creates unordered list.
<ol>Creates ordered list.
<li>Makes a list element.
<div>A tag meant for dividing a page into sections and being styled in CSS.

&lt;Makes <
